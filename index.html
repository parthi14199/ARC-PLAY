Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How much will my project cost?
We are proud to offer high-quality projects done at an affordable price. Because every business has unique needs, we don't use strict "one-size-fits-all" pricing. Instead, we customize our quotes based on the scope and features of your project to ensure you get the best value for your budget. Reach out to us via the Enquiry form for a free, customized quote!
How long does it take to build a website?
The timeline depends entirely on the complexity of the project. A standard business landing page can be completed in 1-2 weeks, while a more complex e-commerce or custom web application may take 4-8 weeks. We will provide a precise timeline during our initial consultation.
Do you provide ongoing support after the project is launched?
Absolutely! We don't just build and disappear. We offer ongoing maintenance, security updates, and technical support packages to ensure your digital assets remain fast, secure, and up-to-date long after launch.
Do I need to provide the content and images?
Yes, you are the expert on your business! We rely on you to provide the text copy and specific brand images. However, if you need assistance, our graphic design team can help create stunning visual assets and icons tailored for your new site.
